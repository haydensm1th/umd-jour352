Swinging into Popularity:
Golf's Unexpected Revival


By Hayden Smith

An activity once perceived exclusive to the rich and snobby, golf has quickly become one of the fastest-growing sports in America


Driving $101.7 billion in economic activity throughout the U.S. in 2022, an increase of 20% over its $84.1 billion impact in 2016 according to a study by the American Golf Industry Coalition, new event venues such as TopGolf have made the game not only more enjoyable but more accessible as well
